Trivia

Original Nocturne Original Nocturne

    "Embrace the darkness", "Cross over", "Cut them from this world!", and "Suffer eternally!" might be referencing The Shrike from Hyperion Cantos by Dan Simmons.
    "Afraid of the dark?" resembles "Afraid of the dark? Wise... " "Afraid of the dark? Wise... "
    "Weather forecast for tonight: dark, with a chance of pain!" might referencing The Hippy-Dippy Weatherman by George Carlin.
    If Nocturne is hit by Smoke Screen Smoke Screen the enemy Graves Graves will say "I've got your darkness" or "Who's in the dark now?" (this was the first time unique dialogue triggered by champion interactions).

Eternum Nocturne Eternum Nocturne

    "We transcend time" might be referencing The Shrike from Hyperion Cantos by Dan Simmons and/or Sovereign from Mass Effect ("We have no beginning. We have no end. We are infinite")
    "We have learned of spin to win spin to win, None can stop--- it was a trick!" references the eponymous meme the eponymous meme and maybe Gial Ackbar from Star Wars ("It's a trap!")
    "Their existence is a mistake", "The end of days draws near", and "We are one" might be referencing Sovereign from Mass Effect ("Organic life is nothing but a genetic mutation, an accident", "The time of our return is coming", "We are legion")
    "Fate cannot be averted" and "We are one" might be referencing Harbinger from Mass Effect ("You cannot escape your destiny" and "We fight as one")
