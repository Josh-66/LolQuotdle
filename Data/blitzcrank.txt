Trivia

Original Blitzcrank Original Blitzcrank

     
       "Exterminate. Exterminate." references the Dalek from Doctor Who as well as the tendency for movie evil robots to say the same.
     
       "A rolling golem gathers no rust." references the adage "A rolling stone gathers no moss".
     
       "The magic calls to me." is nearly identical to  
       Veigar OriginalSquare.png "The magic, it calls to me!"

Lancer Paragon Blitzcrank Lancer Paragon Blitzcrank

    There is one dedicated line ( 
       "Magic and steam guide me.") that appears to have been scrapped from Classic voiceover.

Space Groove Blitz & Crank Space Groove Blitz & Crank

    The dialogue personalities of both cats, Blitz & Crank, closely resemble Pinky & Brain, respectively.
